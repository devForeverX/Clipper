Ever had a moment on your screen you wish you could go back and save? With this clipper, you can. It automatically keeps the last 15 seconds up to 15 minutes of your screen (including internal audio) in memory.

Whenever something worth saving happens, just press **F8**, and the program will instantly turn those past moments into a clip.

Perfect for catching game highlights, funny moments, or anything you didn’t expect but don’t want to lose.

**Note:** This project is currently in beta. Features may change, and you might encounter bugs or performance issues. Feedback and suggestions are highly appreciated!
